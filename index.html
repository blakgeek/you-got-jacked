BlakJax

The goal of BlakJax is to capture five connecting squares horizontally, vertically or diagonally before your opponent can.

The rules are almost just like One Eyed Jack or Sequence. The difference is that there are no one or two eyed jacks. Instead red jacks are used to free up a square that has been captured by your opponent and black jacks are wild which means they can be used to capture any open square except for the jokers.

The cards at the top are yours to play. When its your turn click of your cards to select it. The selected card will be highlighted in green. To capture a square click it when the matching card or a jack is selected. If the play is valid the cell will change to blue signifying that it has been captured by you.

The last four cards that have been played are displayed below your cards. If you have a dead card, meaning all of the squares where it could have been played are already occupied, you can discard it and get a new one by clicking these cards.
