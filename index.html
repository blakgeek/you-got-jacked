          The Board

          The board is made up of 100 cells arranged in a 10x10 grid. Each card in the deck two matching cells on the board expect for jokers which have four and jacks which have none.

          Your Hand

          Your hand shows the cards that you can use to capture or free cells.

          The Discard Pile

          The discard pile shows the last four cards played.

          The Player List

          The player list shows the name and color for each player.

          Capturing Cells

          Click on any of your cards to select it. Then click on a matching free cell to capture it. A captured cell will change to the captor's color.

          Free Jacking

          Free jacking is using a jack of diamonds or jack of hearts to free up a previously captured cell. You cannot free jack a joker.

          Cell Jacking

          Cell jacking is using a jack of clubs or jack of spades to capture any open any open cell on the board. You cannot cell jack a joker.

          Win Left To Right!

          Winning is simple. Be the first player to capture five cells in a line and you win.

          Win Up and Down!

          Winning at You Got Jacked is simple. Be the first player to capture five cells in a line and you win.

          Win Diagonally!

          Winning at You Got Jacked is simple. Be the first player to capture five cells in a line and you win.

          Jokers


          Joker cells that have not been captured can be used by any player to complete a line. After a joker cell is captured by a player it can only be used to complete that player's lines.

          Jokers cannot be free jacked or cell jacked.

          I See Dead Cards

          x

          Dead cards are cards that cannot be played because all of the matching cells have already been captured. These cards can be discard and replace with fresh ones from the deck. This is done by clicking the dead card in your and then either dragging it to the discard pile or clicking the discard pile.

          You can only do this once per play and you have to do it before capturing a cell.
