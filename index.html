The rules of BlakJax are almost just like Sequence but there are no one or two eyed jacks. Instead red jacks are used to free up a square that has been captured by your opponent and black jacks are wild and can be played in any open square except for the corners.

Your hand is located in the upper right section of the screen. When its your turn to play click a card in your hand to select it. The selected card will be highlighted in green. To capture a square click it when the matching card or a jack is selected in your haand. If the play is valid the cell will change to blue signifying that has been captured by you.

If you capture five connecting squares horizontally, vertically or diagonally before the computer you win. If not you lose.

The cards at bottom show the last four cards that have been played.
